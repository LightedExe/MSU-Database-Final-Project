Our Company

Boom Shawarma Bookings is a pioneering technological venture based in the vibrant city of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Established in 2023, our company has quickly risen as a trailblazer in revolutionizing the way individuals book their flights across the globe.

At Boom Shawarma Bookings, we pride ourselves on our innovative approach to flight reservations, drawing inspiration from the fusion of technology and culinary excellence. Our unique name pays homage to the idea that just as shawarma wraps blend various flavors into a delightful experience, our booking platform seamlessly weaves together different travel options to create unforgettable journeys for our users.

As a Malaysian-born enterprise, we are committed to infusing the spirit of diversity, efficiency, and warmth into every aspect of our flight booking system. Our team comprises tech enthusiasts, travel aficionados, and creative minds dedicated to crafting an intuitive and user-centric platform that simplifies the complexities of flight reservations.
